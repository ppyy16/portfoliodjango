Welcome to Photo Mini-Market!

In this project, me and a group of classmates and friends decided to create image manipulation "filters" by using seam carving and Voronoi diagrams. I will only be speaking about Voronoi as that was my contribution to the project.

Initially, my idea of coding and manipulating this algorithm to work for image modification was to only gather points which have high entropy, meaning that they would capture the significant parts of the image where data retention is neccesary. After making this somewhat work, I realized that the image simply did not look like the original enough and realized that a randomized set of points would do just as well, if not better at preserving the image as well as reducing the error in detecting points of entropy in the first place which resulted in the final program.
